What Are Martial Arts?

Martial arts are diverse combat systems and training disciplines utilized for self-defense, fitness, competition, discipline, sport, personal growth, and mental development. They come from cultures all around the world and emphasize different skills such as striking, grappling, weaponry, and mental focus.

Popular Categories:

  • Striking
    • Focuses on punches, kicks, knees, and elbows. Examples include Boxing, Muay Thai, Taekwondo, and Karate.
  • Grappling
    • Centers on throws, takedowns, joint locks, and chokes. Examples include Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu (BJJ), Judo, and Wrestling.
  • Mixed Martial Arts (MMA)
    • Combines multiple disciplines to create a complete, well-rounded combat system used in modern sport fighting.
  • Traditional
    • systems of combat that emphasize cultural heritage, philosophical development, and structured pedagogy over modern sport application
