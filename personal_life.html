Personal Life

My name is Daniil Avdusenko. I'm 14 years old and I'm a freshman at Lane Tech College Prep High School. I was born in Donietsk, a city in Eastern Ukraine, which is about 50 miles from Russian border. In 2014, when Russia occupied Donietsk, my family and I moved to the capital of Ukraine - Kyiv. When I was 4 years old, I started doing Taekwondo in one of the best Ukrainian Taekwondo clubs - "Kycko Fight Club". When the war started, I was 10 years old. In August of 2022, me and my family moved to Olney IL, a little town in Southern Illinois. We've lived there for 3 years, and I found a new Taekwondo club - Master Robert's Taekwondo and in July of this year moved to Chicago. I've found a new Taekwondo and Kickboxing club called "MMA Goal" in Arlington Heights, IL which is Ukrainian. Right now I'm working as a coach for white belts there. I've been doing Taekwondo for 9 years, and I am 5 times Ukrainian National Champion, and 3 times USA National Champion, and have won many other tournaments. Since I've lived in United States for over 3 years now, I'm trilingual - I fluently speak Russian, because I was born in Eastern Ukraine, and everyone speaks Russian there, Ukrainian, and English.



Kychko Team

I joined Kychko Team in September of 2016, when I was 4 years old, and this is a place where I grew up, because I trained here almost everyday, this Team has taken me through most of my life and has given me a lot, because thanks to my coach, Artem Kychko, I know everything I do right now in Taekwondo, and I get recognized by famous people in Taekwondo, who have won many world tournaments.

Master Robert's Taekwondo

After moving to United States, I joined Taekwondo Master Robert's in October of 2022. I was part of this team for 3 years, until I moved to Chicago in July of 2025. This team showed me the American Taekwondo, which is a little different from Ukrainian, and it was a fun and good exprience. I learned a lot of new stuff at this school, and I'm thankfull for being part of it
